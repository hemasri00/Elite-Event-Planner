About Our Company

Discover the passion, creativity, and core leadership team behind Elite Event Planner.

Designing Beautiful Memories Since 2012

Elite Event Planner started with a singular, clear aspiration: to redefine how families and businesses host their special celebrations. What began as a local boutique decorations vendor in Manhattan has flourished into a full-service, award-winning event management company serving luxury events nationwide.

We coordinate everything from floral backdrops and majestic stages to gourmet menus and top acoustic arrays. Our detail-oriented planners work tirelessly behind the scenes so that you can entirely immerse yourself in your milestone moments.
